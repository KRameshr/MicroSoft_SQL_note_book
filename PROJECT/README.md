# AdventureWorks Customer & Sales Analysis

## Project Overview
This SQL project focuses on querying and analyzing the **AdventureWorks2012** database to extract actionable insights about customers and sales. The goal is to transform raw transactional data into meaningful business information for reporting and decision-making.

---

## Problem Statement
How to get details about customers and their sales by querying a large relational database?  
The project addresses:
- Extracting customer contact information (name, email, phone, phone type)
- Analyzing sales for specific months and yearly trends
- Calculating total and month-on-month sales
- Retrieving total sales for individual customers

---

## Highlights
- Understanding table structures, relationships, and data types  
- Using SQL operators, joins, aggregation functions, and date filters  
- Querying real-world transactional data for insights

---

## Tasks Performed
1. Restored the AdventureWorks2012 database in SQL Server.
2. Queried customer contact details from Person, Email, and Phone tables.
3. Extracted sales header and detail records for May 2011.
4. Calculated total sales for May 2011.
5. Generated month-wise sales totals for the year 2011.
6. Calculated total sales for a specific customer (e.g., Gustavo Achong).

---

## Technical Implementation
- **Database:** SQL Server (AdventureWorks2012)  
- **SQL Concepts Used:**  
  - JOINs (INNER, LEFT)  
  - Aggregation functions (SUM, COUNT)  
  - GROUP BY and ORDER BY  
  - Filtering with date functions (YEAR(), MONTH())  
  - Retrieving specific customer transactions

---

## Learning Outcomes
- Practical experience with querying a **large relational database**.  
- Ability to analyze transactional data and generate business insights.  
- Strengthened SQL skills: joins, aggregation, filtering, and data analysis.
